Reasoning:
For ru-RU, the visual system should feel practical, controlled, direct, and PDF-native. A dark matte base with controlled PDF-red accents gives the listing a clear PDF cue without making the design feel aggressive or visually noisy.

The red accent should be used only as restrained emphasis: subtle depth behind the “after” PDF mockup, before/after indicators, small trust markers, thin lines, or controlled highlights.

General visual rules:
