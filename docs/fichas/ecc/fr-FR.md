## Market Angle Decision

Market angle selected:
Remplacer ou corriger une couverture EPUB simplement, avec traitement local et enregistrement d’une nouvelle copie.

Why this angle should convert in this locale:
For fr-FR, the listing should feel clear, polished, and practical. The user should not feel they are downloading a complex ebook editor or an artistic cover-design app. The strongest message is that an EPUB cover can be replaced quickly with a custom image, while keeping the file private.

Primary user desire:
Remplacer une couverture EPUB absente, incorrecte, trop simple ou peu attrayante par une image choisie par l’utilisateur.

Secondary trust proof:
Traitement sur l’appareil, aucun compte, aucun envoi de fichiers, aperçu avant l’enregistrement et création d’une nouvelle copie.

What should NOT be the first screenshot:
Paramètres, bibliothèque vide, détails techniques du fichier, interface d’éditeur d’image générique ou promesse de confidentialité sans montrer la transformation de couverture.

## Visual System

Palette:

* secondary background: #242126
* accent: #9E5A52
* optional support accent: #C98C84
* headline color: #FFFFFF
* subline color: #DDD4DC
* bullet/support text color: #EAB4AD

Reasoning:
For fr-FR, the visual system should feel refined, calm, editorial, and practical. The dark base keeps the product trustworthy and focused, while the muted rose/brown accent gives a softer and more polished feel than a strong red or bright blue.

The accent should be used only for restrained emphasis: thin lines, before/after indicators, small proof points, subtle dividers, or a controlled highlight near the final cover.

General visual rules:
Keep the copy area dark, matte, and readable. The transformation must remain the main proof: couverture faible ou incorrecte → couverture personnalisée plus soignée.

Avoid bright panels behind text, aggressive red glow, excessive decoration, AI sparkle effects, cloud/upload icons, PDF visuals, Kindle-style device positioning, or visuals that make the app look like a generic image editor.
