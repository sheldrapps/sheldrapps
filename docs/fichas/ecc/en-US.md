## Visual System

Palette:

* primary background: #101418
* secondary background: #1A2330
* accent: #4A78A8
* optional support accent: #6FA3D8
* headline color: #FFFFFF
* subline color: #C9D7E6
* bullet/support text color: #DCE6F2

Reasoning:
For en-US, the visual system should feel modern, trustworthy, practical, and utility-tech. A charcoal + slate navy base with steel-blue accents communicates reliability and privacy better than a flat black/gray or red-heavy system.

The blue accent should be used only as controlled emphasis: subtle depth behind devices, small trust markers, before/after indicators, thin lines, or restrained highlights.

Warmth should come mainly from the “after” cover artwork, not from the background system.

General visual rules:
Keep the copy area dark, matte, and readable. The cover transformation must remain the main visual proof: weak/plain cover → polished custom cover.

Avoid bright panels behind text, neon blue glow, generic stock-photo energy, AI sparkle effects, cloud/upload symbols, PDF visuals, Kindle-like device positioning, or anything that makes the app look like a general image editor.
