# Product

## Register

product

## Users

People creating or modifying ebook and cover assets inside Ionic + Angular apps. They are usually trying to pick a device model, choose a cover source, preview the result, adjust it, and then save or export without losing context.

## Product Purpose

Provide focused cover-creation and cover-change workflows that feel dependable, fast, and familiar. The UI should help users complete a task with minimal friction while keeping advanced options available when needed.

## Brand Personality

Clear, practical, calm.

The interface should feel trustworthy, efficient, and unobtrusive. It should reduce cognitive load and let the task stay front and center.

## Anti-references

Over-decorated controls, inconsistent component vocabularies, gratuitous motion, heavy visual treatment on inactive states, and custom affordances that obscure standard task flows.

## Design Principles

- Show only the actions that are meaningful at the current step.
- Keep state changes legible without overwhelming the user.
- Prefer familiar controls and consistent spacing over novelty.
- Make preview, edit, and export states feel like one coherent flow.
- Preserve density where it helps, but keep the hierarchy calm and readable.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Aim for strong contrast, visible focus states, clear labels, and reduced-motion support. Keep touch targets comfortable and ensure state is communicated without relying on color alone.
